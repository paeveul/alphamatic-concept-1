What We Offer

Our Services

Professional air conditioning services across the Klang Valley.

Six Ways We Serve You

01

Air Conditioning Installation

We supply and install split-unit, cassette, and ceiling-mounted aircon systems for homes and offices. Our technicians assess your space and recommend the right capacity. All major brands available — Daikin, Panasonic, Midea, Samsung, and more.

WhatsApp for This Service
02

Servicing & Maintenance

Regular servicing extends the life of your unit and keeps it running at full efficiency. We clean filters, wash coils, clear drainage, and check refrigerant levels. Recommended every 3–4 months for residential, monthly or bi-monthly for heavy commercial use.

WhatsApp for This Service
03

Chemical Wash

A chemical wash goes deeper than a standard service, dissolving mould, bacteria, and stubborn buildup inside the indoor unit. It restores airflow, eliminates odours, and can significantly improve cooling performance. Recommended when regular servicing no longer restores cooling.

WhatsApp for This Service
04

Gas Top-Up (Refrigerant)

Low refrigerant is one of the most common causes of poor cooling. Our technicians perform a pressure check to confirm the issue and top up the gas to the correct level. We also check for leaks to prevent the problem from recurring.

WhatsApp for This Service
05

Aircon Repair

From water leaks and electrical faults to compressor issues and control board failures — our technicians diagnose and fix all types of aircon problems. We carry common replacement parts and can complete most repairs on the same visit.

WhatsApp for This Service
06

Commercial & Office Aircon

We handle installation, servicing, and maintenance contracts for offices, retail units, restaurants, and other commercial spaces. Flexible scheduling available to minimise disruption to your operations.

WhatsApp for This Service
