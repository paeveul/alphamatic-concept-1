Our Story

Who We Are

Alphamatic Airconditioning is a Klang Valley-based HVAC specialist with years of hands-on experience servicing homes, offices, and commercial premises. We built this business on one principle: do the job right, the first time, every time.

From our base in the Klang Valley, we've grown to cover the full region — from Shah Alam and Subang Jaya to Cheras, Ampang, and beyond. Whether it's a single-split unit or a multi-system commercial installation, our team shows up prepared and leaves nothing behind but cool, clean air.

We work with all major brands — Daikin, Panasonic, Midea, Carrier, York, Hitachi, and more — so you're never locked in to one supplier.
